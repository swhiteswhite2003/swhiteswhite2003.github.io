Contact Us

Found a bug, spotted a number that looks off, or want a calculator we haven't built yet? Tell us — feedback is how these tools get better.

Email us

The fastest way to reach us is by email:

hello@swhiteswhite2003.github.io

We read every message and aim to reply within a few business days. To help us help you faster, please include:

  • Which calculator you were using (for example, the mortgage or debt payoff page).
  • What you entered and what result you expected versus what you saw.
  • Your device and browser, if you're reporting something that looks broken.

What we can and can't help with

We're glad to fix bugs, clarify how a calculation works, and take requests for new tools. What we can't do is give you personalised financial, tax, or legal advice — we don't know your full situation, and we're not licensed to. For decisions that matter, please speak with a qualified professional. See our disclaimer for the full picture.

A note on privacy

Anything you put in an email to us is, of course, sent to us. But the numbers you type into the calculators themselves never are — they're processed entirely in your browser and never reach our servers. You can read more on our privacy page.

MoneyMath provides general information only and is not financial advice.

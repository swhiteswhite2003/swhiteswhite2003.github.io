Free, private, and instant

Every calculator on MoneyMath is completely free and runs entirely in your browser. There is nothing to install and nothing to sign up for. The numbers you type — your salary, your balances, your rate — are never uploaded, stored, or shared. When you close the tab, they are gone.

Built on one audited math engine

All twelve tools share a single, unit-tested calculation core, so the payment formula behind the mortgage page is the exact same one behind the auto and personal loan pages. That means consistent, trustworthy results no matter which calculator you reach for — and answers that update the instant you change a number.

Which calculator do you need?

  • Buying or refinancing a home? Start with the Mortgage Calculator to see principal, interest and your full payoff schedule.
  • Financing a car? The Auto Loan Calculator handles down payment and trade-in for you.
  • Borrowing for anything else? The Personal Loan Calculator shows the true cost of the loan.
  • Investing or saving? Watch your money grow with the Compound Interest, Retirement, 401(k) and CD calculators.
  • Saving toward a target? The Savings Goal Calculator tells you exactly how much to set aside each month.
  • Sizing up an investment? Use the ROI Calculator for total and annualized return, or the Simple Interest Calculator for flat, non-compounding interest.
  • Paying down balances? The Debt Payoff Calculator compares snowball and avalanche, and the Credit Card Payoff Calculator shows how fast a fixed payment clears your card.

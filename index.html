Free, private, and instant

Every calculator on MoneyMath is completely free and runs entirely in your browser. There is nothing to install and nothing to sign up for. The numbers you type — your salary, your balances, your rate — are never uploaded, stored, or shared. When you close the tab, they are gone.

Built on one audited math engine

All six tools share a single, unit-tested calculation core, so the payment formula behind the mortgage page is the exact same one behind the auto and personal loan pages. That means consistent, trustworthy results no matter which calculator you reach for — and answers that update the instant you change a number.

Which calculator do you need?

  • Buying or refinancing a home? Start with the Mortgage Calculator to see principal, interest, taxes and insurance.
  • Financing a car? The Auto Loan Calculator handles down payment and trade-in for you.
  • Borrowing for anything else? The Personal Loan Calculator shows the true cost of the loan.
  • Investing or saving? Use the Compound Interest and Retirement calculators to watch your money grow.
  • Paying down balances? The Debt Payoff Calculator compares snowball and avalanche head-to-head.
